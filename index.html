All about me.

Hello! My name is James! Like most people, I am many things! A father, husband, Army veteran, previous business owner but forever- someone who loves to experience things.
I love reading, mostly fantasy, but on occasion sci-fi finds a way to dig into my mind. Robert A Heinlein springs to mind, especially "Starship Troopers" and
"The Moon is a Harsh Mistress". Though a more modern favorite of mine would be, "Children of Time" by Adrian Tchaikovsky, which I highly recommend.
For fun I try to make a habit of going to the gym, which I do enjoy at least half the time. But I definitely prefer more time getting lost in video games that
either give me the freedom to create my own narrative, apply a subtle touch of narrative that complements gameplay, or an asymmetrical/social multiplayer game.
I'm big into social science and psychology so I love the concept of players having different win states and rules.

Two truths and a lie.

1. I love urban exploration and have been to a few abandoned asylums in Massachusetts.
2. I can bench press more than my body weight.
3. I have 30 minutes of logged flight time piloting a helicopter.
